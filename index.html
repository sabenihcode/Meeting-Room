Projector Connection Guide

Two methods: Wireless & Wired

⚡
This is the fastest method for wireless connection!

Press Windows + K

On your keyboard, press Windows + K to open the 'Connect' panel.

Select Projector

Choose the meeting room projector from the list that appears.

Windows Connect Panel showing available devices

Connect the Cable

Connect the HDMI/VGA cable from your laptop to the corresponding port on the projector.

HDMI VGA

Select Input Source

Use the projector remote to select the input source (HDMI/VGA). If the screen doesn't appear, press Windows + P and select 'Duplicate'.

Verify Connection

Your laptop display should now appear on the projector screen.

⚠️
Important Reminder Don't forget to turn off the projector after use to save energy and extend its lifespan!

Kandao Meeting Pro Camera

360° AI-powered conference camera

Power On & Connect

Press the power button, then connect the USB cable from the camera's 'USB OUT' port to your laptop.

Select Camera in App

Open your meeting app (Zoom/Teams), go to Video settings, and select Kandao Meeting Pro.

Connect HDMI & Power On

Connect the HDMI cable from the camera to the projector, then turn on both devices.

Connect to WiFi

Use the remote to access settings and connect the camera to the WiFi network.

FAQ & Troubleshooting

Quick solutions to common problems

Ensure the cable is securely connected, the input source on the projector is correct, and try pressing Windows + P then select 'Duplicate'.

Try a different USB port, ensure "Kandao Meeting Pro" is selected in audio & video settings, and check that no other apps are using the camera.

The most common cause is dead batteries. Try replacing them first. Also ensure there are no obstacles between the remote and camera.
